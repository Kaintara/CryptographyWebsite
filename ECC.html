Elliptic Curve Cryptography

Elliptic Curves are used to create digital signatures that ensure authenticity in the ownership of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin. This is due to a special property of Elliptic Curves that not only creates a harder version of the discrete logarithm problem but also generates significantly smaller private/public keys.
